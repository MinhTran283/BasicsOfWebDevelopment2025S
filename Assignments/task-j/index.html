About UrbanRide

UrbanRide is a fictional bike rental company designed for modern city life. We focus on simple, reliable, and comfortable rides, so you can move around the city without worrying about traffic, parking, or maintenance.

Whether you are a student, a remote worker, or a visitor, our bikes help you cover the "last kilometer" in a sustainable way. Just pick your bike, unlock it, and ride — it's that simple.
